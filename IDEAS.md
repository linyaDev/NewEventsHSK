# Event Ideas / Идеи для событий

## Tribal raids on industrial+ colonies

How would a tribal colony attack a technologically superior settlement?

### 1. Advanced weapon stash
Tribals traded or found a stash of advanced weapons and armor. A tribal raid arrives equipped with industrial-grade gear — flak vests, charge rifles, etc. Rare but dangerous.

### 2. War beast stampede
Tribals trained a herd of war beasts — Mammoths, Megabadgers, Rhinos. The animals charge first, soaking up bullets, while tribal warriors follow behind.

### 3. Tribal mechanitor
A tribal accidentally got hold of a mechlink and discovered a hidden ruin full of dormant mechanoids. Now they control a small army of mechs. Tribal + mechanoid mixed raid.

### 4. Insectoid queen
Tribals tamed an insectoid queen. The raid arrives with waves of insects — megaspiders, spelopedes — directed by tribal handlers. The queen stays in the back spawning reinforcements.

### 5. Combat dryads
Tribals with Gauranlen connection trained a squad of combat dryads — Barkskin, Timber, Clawer variants. A nature-themed assault with fast regenerating units.
